Alhilal Club:-

Al Hilal Saudi Football Club, commonly known as Al Hilal, is one of the most successful and prestigious football clubs in Asia.

Founded in 1957 and based in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia, the club has a rich history of dominance in both domestic and international competitions.

Al Hilal holds the record for the most Saudi Pro League titles, along with multiple King's Cup, Crown Prince Cup, and Super Cup victories.

Internationally, the club has won several AFC Champions League titles, making it one of the most decorated teams in Asia.

Known for its passionate fan base and star-studded squad, Al Hilal continues to be a powerhouse in football, attracting top talents from around the world.
